Cardiology

Cardiac diseases are among the greatest public health challenges of our time. They are very common, costly, disabling and deadly. In recent years, improved understanding of cardiac diseases has led to new and effective treatments, offering fresh hope to many patients. Cardiology department provides comprehensive clinical services, offering a range of diagnostic and therapeutic facilities :

Diagnostic and Therapeutic procedures & Services

Computerized ECG

Exercise Tolerance Test(ETT)

24 Hours ECG (Holter Monitoring)

2D, M Mode, Color Doppler Transthoracic Echo
